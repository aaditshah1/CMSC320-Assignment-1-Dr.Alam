Q2: What field could data science be applied to? Give and Example and how it might help

Answer: Data science can be applied to many fields, like finance, healthcare, etc.
In healthcare, redictive analytics can be used to identify patients at high risk of developing 
chronic conditions, such as diabetes or heart disease. By analyzing patterns in patient data, healthcare 
providers can implement preventative measures or early interventions, potentially reducing 
hospitalizations and improving overall health outcomes.
